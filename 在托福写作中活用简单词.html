在托福写作中活用简单词

  1. 我们不应对我们不了解的文化抱有偏见和怀疑

    • harbor: n.海港，港口；v.持有或怀有
    • We should not harbor prejudice（偏见） or suspicion to cultures we do not understand.
    • We harbor the opinion that...

  2. 只有汇集它们的资源，政府才能有希望战胜全球变暖。

    • pool: n.池子，池塘；v.汇集
    • Only by pooling their resources can governments hope to overcome global warming.
    • Only by pooling the educational resources can government provide high quality education for people in China.

  3. 当今社会的许多问题不是新的，而是映射了与过去相似的困境。

    • mirror: n. 镜子；v.反映
    • Many of today's social problems are not new but mirror similar dilemmas(进退两难的困境) from the past.
    • The serious traffic jams in Beijing mirror the dilemmas remain to solve urgently.
    • 其次考虑再reflict

  4. 考试中的高分掩饰了许多学生并不具有用第二语言有效交流的能力。

    • mask: n.面具；v.掩饰
    • High test scores mask many student's inability to communicate effectively in a second language.
    • A variety of advertisement mask some unrealistic functions of the products.
    • 其次再考虑cover

  5. 人类达成国际和平的能力取决于我们发现共同点的能力。

    • hinge：n. 合页，枢纽；v. 取决于
    • Humanity's ability to achieve international peaces hinges on our ability to find common ground.

  6. 为了缩小贫富差距，政府必须改善社会福利。

    • bridge the gap: 缩小
    • Governments must improve social welfare(社会福利) in ordor to bridge the gap between the rich and poor.
    • 其次考虑narrow，reduce

  7. 大学教育应当塑造思想。开阔眼界。

    • shape: n.形状；vt.塑造
    • University education should shape the mind and broaden horizons(开阔眼界).

  8. 每个国家需要量体裁衣地定制出满足他们各自国情的解决方法。

    • tailor: n. 裁缝；v.使合适，定制
    • Each country needs to tailor a solution which meets their own particular situation.

  9. 通过利用太阳能和风能，我们能让社会更加环保。

    • harness: n.马具；v.利用
    • By harnessing solar and wind energy, we can help our societies become more "green".

  10. 为了抑制学生对电脑游戏的热衷，父母和学校应该提供另一种健康的替代方案。

    • curb: n.勒马索；v.控制，约束
    • In order to curb students' appetite(胃口，性质) for computer games, parents and schools should come up with healthy alternatives(替代).

每天背诵三遍，来来回回翻来覆去。
